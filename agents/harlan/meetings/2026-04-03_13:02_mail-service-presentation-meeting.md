# Mail service presentation meeting

## Summary

## Summary

Jason led a sales presentation with KDOT (Kansas Department of Transportation) public involvement staff to discuss full-service mailing solutions for their transportation project outreach. The meeting followed a previous call that hadn't met their needs, but this presentation was well-received. KDOT needs help with postcard mailings for various projects (K30, K33, K5) ranging from 50-2,000 pieces, targeting specific geographic areas around project corridors in counties like Leavenworth and Wyandotte. Jason explained their capabilities including Every Door Direct mailings, address list procurement, data processing with move update suppression, IMB tracking options, and fast turnaround times (3-4 days). The KDOT team was particularly interested in avoiding past issues with PO box-only towns and getting better delivery confirmation than their current state mailing house provides.

## Atmosphere

Professional, collaborative, and optimistic. The KDOT team was engaged and enthusiastic, explicitly noting this meeting went "way better than our other one" and that Jason's company "actually have what we are looking for." There was a consultative back-and-forth with genuine problem-solving around their specific pain points.

## Key Takeaways

- KDOT needs full-service postcard mailing for 12-18+ projects annually, with volumes typically 50-2,000 pieces per project
- Current pain points: slow turnaround with state mailing house, PO box delivery issues in small towns, lack of tracking/confirmation
- Jason's company offers 3-4 day turnaround, address verification, Every Door Direct at 23¢ postage, and optional IMB tracking (+2¢) with portal access
- Can suppress PO boxes when needed, but small towns with PO box-only delivery require special handling
- KDOT will email specific project files (K33 with ~300 addresses, Leavenworth/Wyandotte EDDM project) for sample pricing
- Contract structure needs clarification—Jason will contact Kansas purchasing to determine best existing state contract or project-based threshold approach

## Action Items

- **Jason**: Follow up with KDOT team next week with pricing estimates for K33 targeted mailing and Leavenworth/Wyandotte EDDM project
- **KDOT team**: Email project files and specifications to Jason for sample quotes, including postcard dimensions and mailing lists
- **KDOT team**: Provide annual project volume estimates and clarify procurement threshold limits on their end
