## Summary

## Summary

Conducted a product demonstration and training session for City of Olathe staff on Lineage's online certified mail processing system. The meeting was organized by Greg from the city and included participants from multiple departments including the City Clerk's office (Brenda), Housing Authority, and other city departments. Walked through the full platform workflow including creating mailings, envelope selection, tracking capabilities, bulk upload functionality, and reporting features. Addressed key concerns about data confidentiality and established that the Housing Authority would need a separate account due to privacy regulations around their beneficiaries.

## Atmosphere

Professional and collaborative with active engagement from participants. Tone was practical and solution-oriented, with attendees asking relevant operational questions and expressing appreciation for the cost savings and efficiency gains.

## Key Takeaways

- **Cost savings confirmed**: Eliminates green card electronic return receipt fees and reduces certified postage costs
- **Account structure**: Most city departments will share one portal with individual logins; Housing Authority requires separate account due to confidentiality requirements
- **System currently in test mode**: Participants can practice creating mailings and uploads without live processing
- **Bulk upload available**: CSV import supported for mass mailings (50-200+ pieces) via profile > control panel > bulk upload
- **Real-time tracking**: Delivery notifications process within 24 hours, not weekly batch updates
- **Only address data stored in system**: Document content never enters the platform
- **Reporting available**: Usage reports by job type, piece count, and department trackable
