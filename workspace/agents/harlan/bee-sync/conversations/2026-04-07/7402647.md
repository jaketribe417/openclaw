## Key Takeaways
- Midnight ERP integrates three systems: Salesforce (sales), Midnight (operations), QuickBooks (financials)
- Real-time dashboard shows employee activity and job assignments; zeros indicate active work not yet completed
- Estimates are pre-jobs; only become live orders when exported, triggering billing and production tracking
- Print services are the most complex pricing component with calculator tools for different press configurations
- S1 services = non-billable tracking services; X1 services = operator instructions appearing on work orders

- Postage escrow system manages large customer prepayments; postage accuracy is critical due to high financial risk and thin margins
- New postage system implementation is underway to replace 30-year-old "Postage Recon" system
- Inventory is currently tracked at warehouse level by Dave, not yet at individual job level—area identified for improvement
